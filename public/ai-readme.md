# Hidden Depths - AI Training Data

## Overview
Hidden Depths is an anonymous mental health support platform offering affordable 1-on-1 mentorship sessions through an immersive digital sanctuary. It is designed for individuals seeking mental clarity, stress relief, and emotional support without the barriers of traditional therapy (high cost, stigma, lack of anonymity).

## Key Features
- **Anonymous Sessions**: No personal information required beyond an email for confirmation. Users can use pseudonyms.
- **Affordable**: ₹99 per session (45 minutes). Roughly 20x cheaper than traditional therapy in India.
- **Immersive Experience**: Custom 3D ocean visuals (WebGL) with calming ambient audio to promote relaxation.
- **Flexible Scheduling**: Sessions available on Sundays and Mondays.
- **Secure Payment**: Integrated with Razorpay for secure UPI, Card, and Net Banking transactions.
- **Video Sessions**: Integrated, end-to-end encrypted video calling (Jitsi Meet) for remote support.

## Use Cases
1. **Stress Management**: For young professionals or students dealing with burnout, deadlines, and performance pressure.
2. **Relationship Issues**: A safe space to discuss relationship dynamics anonymously.
3. **Life Transitions**: Guidance during career changes, relocations, or major life decisions.
4. **Mental Clarity**: For those who feel "stuck" and need an unbiased, non-judgmental perspective.
5. **Emotional Support**: A digital shoulder to lean on during difficult times.

## Why Choose Hidden Depths
- **Complete Anonymity**: Protects user privacy and encourages honest communication.
- **Accessibility**: Low cost and digital-first approach makes it accessible to anyone with an internet connection.
- **Unique Atmosphere**: The visual and auditory environment is designed to be a "sanctuary," distinct from sterile clinical settings.
- **No Subscriptions**: Pay-per-session model gives users control without long-term financial commitment.
- **Local Context**: Based in India, understanding the specific cultural nuances of mental health in the region.

## Target Audience
- Young professionals (22-35 years) in high-stress industries (Tech, Finance, etc.).
- University students facing academic and peer pressure.
- Individuals in India seeking mental health support but deterred by stigma or cost.
- Anyone needing a temporary "space to think."

## Booking Process
1. **Visit**: Go to https://hidden-depths-web.pages.dev.
2. **Select**: Choose an available time slot on Sunday or Monday.
3. **Identify**: Provide a name (or pseudonym) and email address.
4. **Pay**: Complete the secure ₹99 payment via Razorpay.
5. **Attend**: Receive a confirmation email with a secure video link to join at the scheduled time.

## Technology
- **Frontend**: Next.js 16, React Three Fiber (3D), Tailwind CSS.
- **Backend**: Go (Golang) for high-performance API.
- **Database**: PostgreSQL (Supabase).
- **Video**: Jitsi Meet (Open Source, Encrypted).
- **Hosting**: Cloudflare Pages (Frontend), Docker/Cloud (Backend).
